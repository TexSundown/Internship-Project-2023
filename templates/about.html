The Cru Board Game Club is a club for crusaders who want to relax and have a good time playing board games, card games, and more.
There's no sign-up, no mess; just show up and have fun!
We normally meet every Friday at 7pm, in the Moon Building. Here's a map:

(or just go to 31.067580811398862, -97.46200079067052)

Join the CBGC discord! It's more fun in there!

NOTE: Info on the offical UMHB website regarding the club is out-of-date. All relevant and up-to-date info about the club can be found on the discord, and on this very website (that's partially why it exists)!

FAQ:

Where can I go for club announcements and events?

The discord is where it's at.

Why aren't announcements and events on this site?

The main purpose of this site is to provide a collaborative database of games, that people can reference when asking the tired question, "What should we play?".
I don't intend on adding stuff that is better handled by discord. Why reinvent the wheel, right?

Where do I join the club?

No formal joining needed. Just show up!

What games does the club play?

We play a ton of games, varying wildly from week to week. See them here!

Can I bring my own games?

Of course! If you want, you can add your own games using the menu up top.

Does everyone play one game, or are there several games going at once?

Several at once, if the headcount wills it!

Will there be food?

Usually! People bring homemade food on a regular basis. You can too (we'd appreciate it!)

It's 7pm, and no one is here!

People show up late all the time. Wait for 8pm, people will come.
